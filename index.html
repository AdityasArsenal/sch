Academics

Pre-Nursery: Laying the Foundation

  • Play-based learning for holistic development.
  • Focus on early language and numeracy skills.
  • Development of fine and gross motor skills.
  • Nurturing social and emotional growth.
  • Stimulating cognitive development through engaging activities.

Middle School: Building Deeper Understanding

  • Comprehensive learning in core subjects (English, Math, Science, Social Studies).
  • Emphasis on critical thinking and problem-solving.
  • Development of strong communication and collaboration skills.
  • Diverse learning experiences through varied teaching methods.
  • Fostering independence and academic responsibility.

Faculty

At JYANAYOGI, we pride ourselves on providing an all-encompassing curriculum designed to nurture every facet of a child's development. Beyond academic excellence, we foster creativity, critical thinking, and social-emotional growth through a wide array of enriching activities and subjects. Our dedicated team of passionate educators goes above and beyond to create a supportive and engaging learning environment, tailoring their approach to meet the unique needs of each student. Together, we strive to empower our students to become well-rounded individuals, equipped with the knowledge, skills, and character to thrive in an ever-evolving world.
